Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026.

This page explains what cookies alejandroberdonces.com uses, what they're for, and how you can accept, reject, or change your decision at any time.

What is a cookie?

It's a small file that a website stores in your browser to remember information about your visit, such as your preferences or whether you've visited the page before.

What cookies does this site use?

We only use analytics cookies, through Google Analytics 4 (managed with Google Tag Manager). We don't use advertising cookies or third-party tracking beyond Google Analytics.

CookiePurposeDurationOwner
_gaDistinguishes unique users for visit statistics2 yearsGoogle Analytics

These cookies are only installed if you click "Accept" on the cookie notice. If you click "Reject" or don't interact with the notice, no Google Analytics script loads and no analytics cookie is installed.

How do I change my choice?

You can withdraw or give your consent at any time by clicking "Cookie preferences" in the footer of any page on this site. You can also delete already-installed cookies from your browser settings.

Legal basis

This processing is based on your consent (GDPR art. 6.1.a and Spanish LSSI-CE art. 22), which you can withdraw at any time without affecting normal use of the site.
