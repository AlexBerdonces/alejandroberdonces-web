What does this tool do?

You enter a goal and some context, and the tool generates an improved prompt using Google's generative AI model (Gemini). It's a free utility, no sign-up or account required.

What happens to what you write

What you write is sent to Google (Gemini) to generate the prompt. On the free tier we use, Google may use that content to improve its models, including human review in some cases. Don't enter personal data, confidential information, or anything you wouldn't want reaching a third party.

What we store and what we don't

We store, anonymously, the goal, the context, and the generated prompt, along with any rating you give it (optional, 1 to 5 stars). We don't store your IP address, our own cookies, or any data that identifies you. This data is kept for up to 12 months and used only to improve the tool.
